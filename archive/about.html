Logging an infusion

Hemolog's main function is logging infusions. The way Hemolog accomplishes this is by giving you access to a map of the body. Simply touch the part of the body where your bleed occurred and fill in the medical information. You're done!

History

My name is Michael Schultz, and I am the creator of Hemolog. I am a moderate type A hemophiliac and started this project largely for myself. I saw a huge need to speed up the process of logging my infusion information and wanted the benefits of having my logs in a digital format so that I could send them to whomever I wanted instantly.

Benefits

  • Green (Eco-friendly)
  • Digital copies storied on personal computer.
  • Quick access wherever you are.
  • Track results.
  • Secured by password.
  • Email log directly to caretakers.
